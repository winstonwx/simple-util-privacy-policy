Privacy Policy for AgeStory

Last updated: May 1, 2026

AgeStory ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

AgeStory is designed as a personal life and memory utility app. We do not directly collect, store, or share personal information.

Any data you enter—such as names, birthdays, zodiac information, reminders, or notes—is stored locally on your device.

2. Notifications

AgeStory may send local notifications for birthday reminders, countdowns, or scheduled alerts. These notifications are processed on your device and are not sent to external servers.

3. Calendar Sync (Optional Feature)

If you choose to export events (such as birthdays) to your device calendar, AgeStory uses your device’s native calendar system.

We do not access or store your calendar data on external servers.

4. Advertising (If Applicable)

If AgeStory includes ads, we may use Google AdMob, a third-party advertising service provided by Google.

AdMob may automatically collect:

  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Advertising identifiers
  • IP address (for general location)
  • App usage data for ad performance

This data is used to improve ad relevance and performance.

Google Privacy Policy

5. Data Storage

All personal data remains on your device unless you explicitly export or sync it using system features (such as calendar integration).

6. Third-Party Services

AgeStory may use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob (Advertising, if enabled)
  • Device Calendar System (optional sync/export feature)

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

7. Your Privacy Choices

You can manage or limit data usage through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking
  • Calendar permissions: Device Settings → Apps → AgeStory

8. Children's Privacy

AgeStory does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Third-party services may collect limited data as described in their own policies.

9. Data Security

We do not store user data on external servers. All information remains on your device unless shared through system-level features.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
