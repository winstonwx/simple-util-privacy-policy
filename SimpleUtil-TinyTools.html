Privacy Policy for Tiny Tools

Last updated: April 12, 2026

Tiny Tools ("the App", "we", "our", or "us") is developed and maintained by an independent developer. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

1. Information Collection

Tiny Tools is a utility application. We do not require user registration or collect directly identifiable personal information such as name, email, or account data.

However, the App uses third-party services (such as Google AdMob) that may collect certain information for advertising and analytics purposes.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

Tiny Tools uses Google AdMob, a third-party advertising service provided by Google, to display advertisements.

Google AdMob may collect and process the following data:

  • Advertising ID (Google Advertising ID)
  • Device information (device model, operating system version)
  • IP address (used for approximate location)
  • App usage and interaction data (for ad performance and analytics)

This data is used for advertising, ad measurement, analytics, and fraud prevention.

Data collected by Google AdMob is processed according to Google’s Privacy Policy:

Google Privacy Policy

3. Data Usage

We do not collect or store personal data directly. Any data collected is processed by third-party services such as Google AdMob for advertising, analytics, and fraud prevention purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

Tiny Tools uses the following third-party service:

  • Google AdMob (Advertising)

These services may collect data as described in their own privacy policies.

5. Permissions

Tiny Tools does not request sensitive permissions such as location, contacts, or camera access. However, third-party services like AdMob may use standard network access to deliver ads.

6. Your Privacy Choices

You can control personalized advertising through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking

7. Children's Privacy

Tiny Tools is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your region).

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Third-party services such as Google AdMob may collect data as described in their own privacy policies and comply with applicable child safety regulations.

Since we do not collect personal data directly, your information remains on your device. Third-party providers are responsible for securing any data they process.
