Privacy Policy for Tiny Tools

Last updated: April 12, 2026

Tiny Tools ("the App", "we", "our", or "us") is developed and maintained by an independent developer. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

1. Information Collection

Tiny Tools is designed as a simple utility app. We do not directly collect, store, or share personal information.

The App does not require user registration or login. Any data generated within the App remains on your device.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

Tiny Tools uses Google AdMob, a third-party advertising service provided by Google, to display ads.

AdMob may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device information (device type, OS version)
  • Advertising identifiers (e.g., Google Advertising ID)
  • IP address (for approximate location)
  • App interaction data for ad performance

This information is used by Google to provide, personalize, and improve advertisements.

Google Privacy Policy

3. Data Usage

We do not use your personal data. Any data collected is handled by third-party services such as AdMob in accordance with their policies.

4. Third-Party Services

Tiny Tools uses the following third-party service:

  • Google AdMob (Advertising)

These services may collect data as described in their own privacy policies.

5. Permissions

Tiny Tools does not request sensitive permissions such as location, contacts, or camera access. However, third-party services like AdMob may use standard network access to deliver ads.

6. Your Privacy Choices

You can control personalized advertising through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking

7. Children's Privacy

Tiny Tools does not knowingly collect personal information from children. However, third-party services like AdMob may collect data as described in their policies.

8. Data Security

Since we do not collect personal data directly, your information remains on your device. Third-party providers are responsible for securing any data they process.
