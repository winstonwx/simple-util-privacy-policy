Privacy Policy for CrossCal

Last updated: April 12, 2026

CrossCal ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

CrossCal is designed as a simple utility tool. We do not directly collect, store, or share personal information.

All date, time, and time zone data you enter is processed locally on your device and is not sent to our servers.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

CrossCal uses Google AdMob, a third-party advertising service provided by Google, to display ads.

AdMob may collect and use certain information automatically, including:

  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Advertising identifiers (such as Google Advertising ID)
  • IP address (for general location)
  • App usage data for ad performance

This data is used to provide and improve ad relevance and performance.

For more information, please review:

Google Privacy Policy

3. How Information Is Used

We do not use your personal data. Any data collected by AdMob is used by Google for advertising purposes in accordance with their policies.

4. Data Storage

CrossCal does not store personal data on external servers.

Any app-related data remains on your device unless handled by third-party services such as AdMob.

5. Third-Party Services

CrossCal uses the following third-party service:

  • Google AdMob (Advertising)

These services may collect data as described in their own privacy policies.

6. Your Privacy Choices

You can limit personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:

  • On Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads
  • On iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking

7. Children's Privacy

CrossCal does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. However, third-party services like AdMob may collect data as described in their policies.

8. Data Security

We do not collect personal data directly. Third-party services are responsible for securing any data they process.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
